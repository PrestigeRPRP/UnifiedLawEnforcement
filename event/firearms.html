About Firearms Training Course

Master the Fundamentals:

This comprehensive course equips you with the knowledge and skills necessary for safe and responsible firearm ownership in San Andreas. Whether you're a new gun owner or seeking a refresher, this course covers everything you need to know.


Learn the Law & Your Rights:

Understand permit requirements, carry options, and crucial safety rules.


Sharpen Your Skills:

Master the basics of handling, storing, and operating your firearm with confidence.



This course is designed to empower you with the confidence and knowledge to handle firearms safely and responsibly.

What you'll learn:


• Firearm Laws & Regulations: Understand permit requirements, legalities of open vs concealed carry, and your rights and responsibilities as a gun owner in San Andreas.

• Safe Handling & Operation: Master the six basic gun safety rules, learn how to properly operate your firearm, and explore safe storage techniques to prevent unauthorized access.

• Responsible Use: Gain knowledge of situational awareness and responsible gun use practices.

Course Completion Benefits:


• Fulfills the "Certificate of Firearms - Basic Knowledge, Training, and Safety Course" requirement for obtaining a License/Permit to Carry in San Andreas.

• Empowers you to handle firearms with confidence and competence.



San Andreas Permit to Carry:


• Required for carrying a concealed handgun in public.

• Learn about the application process, fees, and renewal procedures.

• Understand the restrictions and responsibilities associated with a Permit to Carry.



Together, let's promote safe and responsible gun ownership in San Andreas!

Frequently Asked Questions:

Cost & Duration of Permit to Carry:

• Police Clearance - $8,000
• Weapon's Clearance - $15,000
• Renewal Fee - $10,000
• Expired Permit: You cannot renew, you'll need to re-apply.
• Stolen Firearm: Report it to the police immediately.
Record the firearm's serial number for reporting purposes.
