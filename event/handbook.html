About Our Handbook

At the heart of our department lies a unwavering commitment to public safety. To ensure the security and well-being of our community, we have established specialized units, each dedicated to specific facets of law enforcement.

Departments

Motorcycle Unit (MCU):
Our MCU officers are highly trained in advanced riding techniques and tactical maneuvers. They are equipped to respond quickly to incidents, conduct traffic enforcement, and provide support to other units.

MCU Handbook

SWAT Team:
Our SWAT team is a specialized unit trained to handle high-risk situations, such as hostage crises, active shooter incidents, and high-risk warrant services.

SWAT Handbook

High-Speed Pursuit Unit (HSU)
The HSU is responsible for safely and effectively conducting high-speed pursuits, utilizing advanced driving techniques and tactical considerations.

SWAT Handbook

Air Support Unit:
Our Air Support Unit provides aerial support to ground units, conducting surveillance, search and rescue operations, and assisting in tactical operations.

ASD Handbook (Soon)

Off-Road Unit:
Our Off-Road Unit is specialized in navigating challenging terrain and responding to incidents in remote areas. They are equipped with specialized vehicles and trained to handle various off-road situations.


Detective
Our Detective Unit is dedicated to investigating complex crimes, collecting evidence, and identifying suspects. They work tirelessly to solve cases and bring criminals to justice.



Through the combined efforts of these specialized units, our department strives to create a safer and more secure community for all.
